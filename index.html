Earthians

Earthians are 10,000 unique algorithmically generated depictions of human traits and characteristics.

As a result, we aimed to develop a society that embodies the future of human diversity and create a world where people of all backgrounds can live in harmony.

An Earthian's unique combination of 500+ attributes makes him or her unmistakably unique and reveals how different human traits can evolve over time.

FAQs

You ask, We Answer
What is an Earthian?

Earthians are 10,000 unique algorithmically generated depictions of human traits and characteristics (both female and male). As a result, we aim to develop a society that embodies the future of human diversity and create a world (Earth 2.0) where people of all backgrounds can live in harmony.

When can I mint my Earthian?

Minting will take place September 24th. We will announce an official  time on here, twitter, and discord in the coming days.

How many Earthians can I mint?

There will be a limit of 1 Earthian per transaction. But you can process as many transactions as you like, as long as there are Earthians left to mint.

What solana wallet can I use?

You will be able to use your Phantom or Sollet wallet to mint an Earthian.

How much does each Earthian cost?

Pre-Sale Price is 0.5 Sol. Mint price is 1 Sol.

How are Earthians generated?

Earthians are generated on demand based on an algorithm that picks from 500+ features to create a unique and never seen before Earthian.

Are there resale royalties?

Yes, a royalty of 5% will be placed on resale. 2% will go towards the original minter (owner) and the rest will be used for the further development of the project.
